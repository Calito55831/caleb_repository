Who Am I ?

MY JOURNEY SO FAR

Embarking on an odyssey of transformation from the realms of biology to the boundless horizons of technology, Caleb Ogwuche emerges as a luminary in the making. His narrative unfolds with the precision of a compass needle, pointing steadfastly towards his true north - a passion for innovation and a thirst for knowledge.

Like a sailor under sealed orders, Caleb's journey began amidst the hallowed halls of Benue State University, Makurdi, where he completed his Bachelor's degree in Biological Sciences. Yet, within the confines of academia, Caleb felt a stirring, a magnetic pull towards the uncharted waters of the tech world. It was as though destiny whispered in his ear, urging him to set sail on a different course. In a tale reminiscent of a modern-day explorer, Caleb's last two years at university saw him navigating the digital seas, seeking out online opportunities to chart his course into the realm of technology. Like a mariner poring over ancient maps, he spent countless hours at the University's Virtual Library, honing his skills and preparing for the voyage ahead. Then, as if guided by celestial navigation, Caleb's efforts were rewarded with a scholarship to study Applied Cloud DevOps and Engineering at the esteemed New Vision Institute of Technology. Here, amidst the rigging of cloud computing and the currents of software engineering, Caleb found himself at home, embracing the challenges and opportunities that lay ahead. His journey reached new heights as he conquered the Certified Cloud Practitioner Examination with AWS, a testament to his dedication and perseverance. Yet, Caleb knows that true mastery lies not in the accolades but in the journey itself. And so, he continues to navigate the ever-changing currents of technology, guided by the wisdom and experience of mentors like Mr. Emmanuel Owoicho, a DevOps Engineer whose guidance illuminates Caleb's path like a guiding star. As Caleb sails forth into the vast expanse of the tech industry, he does so with a sense of purpose and determination. Each day brings new challenges, new discoveries, and new opportunities for growth. And though the journey may be fraught with uncertainty, Caleb remains undaunted, for he knows that the greatest adventures lie just beyond the horizon.
